FAQ

What are shared resources?

Your website shares the memory, computing power and bandwidth of the servers it resides on with others. Another website cannot access your website or the resources it occupies. We set limits based on the web hosting offer you choose and can increase them if we see your website may need a little extra. Bandwidth is shared and we throttle it to make sure everybody get's a fair share. If we see your website constantly needs a lot more, we will advise you and you are free to remain on the plan you have chosen.

What is standard support?

We provide support via email only for standard support and you can expect a response within one hour during normal UK business hours (9am-5pm GMT, Mon-Fri). Our dedicated offer provides support options through a Service Level Agreement tailored to your needs.

What about RAM, CPU and traffic?

We have listened to our customers over the years and they told us that they find figures provided not representative of the actual performance or quality of service. So we decided to not provide them anymore. We set base limits for resources and change them based on individual websites needs.

For example, your website may be performing well with half the RAM allocated to another on the same offer. Both pay for the same performance and quality. Whereas websites on a higher offer may find their websites performance somewhat higher than that of the lower cost one.

For smaller websites with lesser visitors, the standard performance works well and saves money. Larger sites or those with more visitors may want larger amounts of resources and bandwidth to get the same good performance level.

How do you compare with other web hosters?

Other managed website hosting companies will, in most cases, not setup or amend your domain names, email accounts or website software. You will get access to a control panel and knowledge base and some technical support. If you want them to do something for you, you will usually have to pay extra for a 'white gloves' concierge-style hosting service.

You will not need to login to anything or worry about configuration and settings, we will do these things for you,
